Turn strategy into action faster

Since governments across the globe issued the very first wave of stay-at-home orders back in 2020, essential businesses like convenience stores have remained open. You’ve had to find a way to keep the lights on, the shelves stocked, the stores functioning, and people safe.
In the meantime, you and your peers haven’t stood still. You’re continuing to innovate and invest in new ways to serve your customers and grow. Whether it’s new grab-and-go fresh foods, contactless check-out, EV charging stations, or other new initiatives, the pace of innovation is faster than ever before.

At the core to hurdling market challenges as they come, and unlocking new opportunities: Agility.

How do you quickly turn new strategies and initiatives into the right actions on the ground, across every one of your locations?
The Convenience Store AGILITY Summit brings together some of the best operations minds from the multi-unit operator community to answer this question. Our panel of experts will share ideas, new approaches, and best practices that are taking shape in a variety of areas, including:

  • Last-mile services
  • Fresh food offerings & food safety
  • Loss prevention / asset management
  • HR - Risk management
  • Regulatory & compliance
  • COVID-19 protocols
  • Technology & the store of the future
